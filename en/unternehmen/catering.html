Your specialist for catering staff

Banquet service, finger-food reception or a busy production kitchen under pressure: our catering team serves, pours and clears — professionally and with a smile. Service staff, kitchen helpers and runners who keep even full houses running smoothly.

Where we step in across catering.

From the kitchen to logistics: motivated students who pitch in at every station.

Kitchen helper

Mise en place, plating and support at the pass: our kitchen helpers assist your team with prep and keep things running even at high volumes.

Dishwashing helper

At the dishwashing line and the hand-wash station, our dishwashing helpers keep crockery clean and the workflow smooth, so service and kitchen are always ready to restock.

Logistics staff

Set-up and tear-down, goods transport and material handling: our logistics staff get equipment and catering to the location on time and lend a hand on site.

Service staff

Setting tables, serving and clearing: our service staff keep things running smoothly for your guests — attentive, friendly and composed even when the house is full.

Hygiene you don't have to arrange first.

In catering, food hygiene is mandatory. Our staff already bring everything required, so your assignment starts smoothly and on the right side of the law.

  • Completed food-hygiene training
  • Trained in the hygienic handling of food
  • Documented and ready to work right away
