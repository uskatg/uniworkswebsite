Frequently asked questions.

What kind of staffing needs can I cover through uniworks?
With uniworks you can cushion short-term peaks in demand as well as cover longer-term staffing needs. We provide you with temporary staff for individual shifts or for several weeks and months. Our uniworkers are available to you in commercial, administrative and private settings alike. In doing so, we cover a broad spectrum of tasks: from simple jobs requiring little training (e.g. as a warehouse worker, sales assistant or moving helper) through to more demanding positions such as waiter/waitress or cashier.
How quickly can I book staff?
As a rule, an assignment is possible with a lead time of 24 hours, and in individual cases (for example with follow-on bookings) even at shorter notice.
In which regions is uniworks present?
uniworks was founded in Munich and is now present across Germany. This includes cities such as Augsburg, Berlin, Dortmund, Frankfurt am Main, Freiburg, Hamburg, Leipzig, Nuremberg, Stuttgart and Ulm. If you need support in a region that isn't listed, don't hesitate to contact us. Our experience shows that, with a little lead time, this is achievable too.
What fixed costs should I expect?
None. At uniworks there are no monthly fees and no monthly minimum commitment. You pay only for the hours the uniworkers actually work — or, if you cancel within 48 hours of the agreed start time.
I'm very happy with my uniworker — will I generally be assigned the same person again?
To simplify processes and avoid unnecessary onboarding time, we always try to take your preferences into account. You increase the likelihood of being assigned the same person again by giving us feedback on how satisfied you are.
Can I cancel my booking?
In business, short-term changes can always happen, and in principle you can cancel any booking you've made. Please understand that if you cancel within 48 hours of the agreed start time, the respective shift will be invoiced regardless of whether it is used.
