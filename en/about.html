Our story

How did uniworks actually come about?

The idea of bringing students and businesses together grew out of first-hand experience: Moritz, Thommy, David & Stefan founded the company while still studying themselves. They noticed how few options students had to gain flexible, hands-on experience during their studies while also being paid fairly. At the same time, Moritz's experience as an entrepreneur showed just how hard it can be to find qualified workers for your own business.

That realization became a vision that is reality today: students and businesses benefit equally from a flexible model. On one side, attractive pay and a job that fits perfectly around studying. On the other, access to a large talent pool that makes the perfect hire possible for any open role.
