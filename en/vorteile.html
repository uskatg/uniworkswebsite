Your benefits

Discover your benefits as a uniworker!

From the very beginning, our philosophy at uniworks has been not only to create flexible work opportunities for students, but also to recognise commitment and dedication. In this spirit, we’re pleased to announce that uniworkers who work for us five or more times per calendar month are additionally awarded the uniworker plus status. This unlocks new perks — from priority on shifts to thousands of fitness and wellness options in cooperation with Wellpass.

uniworker

Your start at uniworks – from your very first shift.

  • Access to the uniworks app
  • Flexible work opportunities
  • Minimum wage of 16 euros per hour
  • Personal WhatsApp support
From 5 shifts

uniworker plus

Everything from uniworker – plus exclusive extras.

  • Access to the uniworks app
  • Flexible work opportunities
  • Minimum wage of 16 euros per hour
  • Personal WhatsApp support
Additional benefits
  • Notifications for new shifts
  • Priority access to sought-after shifts
  • Priority for jobs with a waiting list
  • Flexible wage advance available
  • On request: Wellpass

Free access to over 8,000 fitness and spa facilities across Germany worth more than €100 (for a €19.90 co-payment).

5+ shifts per calendar month

How do I become a uniworker plus?

Right when you join uniworks, you start with uniworker status and enjoy flexible working hours, an attractive minimum wage and the convenience of the uniworks app. In your profile tab, you can check the number of shifts you’ve completed at any time in the app. As soon as you’ve completed 5 or more shifts in a calendar month, you’ll receive uniworker plus status for the current and the following month.
